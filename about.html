About

Born and raised in Cameroon, I moved to the united states when I was about 13. I attended high school in Washington DC and college in Hanover New Hampshire. Since graduating, I have worked in the therapy industry as a guide in wilderness therapy, I have been a crew lead for a conservation corp and I have travelled across south America and most of Europe. I will be attending graduate school in the fall of 2024 and in the meantime I work at a ski resort and at the boys and girls club while writing in my free time.
